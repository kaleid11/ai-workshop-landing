## Pro Tips

### Successful AI Alignment Principles
1. **Start small, think big** - Quick wins build momentum
2. **Involve the team early** - Co-create solutions, don't impose
3. **Measure everything** - Data drives decisions and proves ROI
4. **Iterate constantly** - AI tools evolve fast, so should you
5. **Focus on outcomes** - Tools are means, not ends

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Implementing too many tools at once
- Skipping training and onboarding
- Not measuring impact
- Ignoring team feedback
- Choosing tools before understanding needs
