1. **Professional Headshot**
   - Clean, well-lit photo in business attire
   - Great for LinkedIn posts and professional content
   - Resolution: At least 1000x1000px
   - ☐ Photo ready

2. **Casual Photos**
   - Natural, relaxed photos in everyday settings
   - Perfect for Instagram and social media
   - Resolution: At least 1000x1000px
   - ☐ Photo ready

3. **Action Shots**
   - Photos of you working, speaking, or in your element
   - Great for showcasing expertise
   - Resolution: At least 1000x1000px
   - ☐ Photo ready

**Photo Quality Tips:**
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1000x1000px)
- Ensure good lighting
- Avoid heavily filtered photos
- Include clear shots of your face
- The AI works best with photos where you're clearly visible and well-lit
